


1.9.5 Recursion (재귀)


[목표]

 - factorial n = {- n! -} 

[팩토리얼 함수의 특성을 분석 ]
============================================
(1) n=1 :  n! = 1                  base 

(2) n>1 : n! =  n x (n-1)!         inductive
============================================
  
n! = n * (n-1) * ... * 2 * 1

(n-1)! = (n-1) * ... * 2 * 1

[하스켈로 작성]

 - factorial 1 = 1
   factorial n = n * factorial (n-1)
